Our Favourite Parks In Brooklyn

Tranquil Strolls
PROSPECT PARK

Prospect Park is a 585-acre park and one of the largest in Brooklyn. It offers a wide range of activities, including biking, hiking, picnicking, and sports. It also features the Brooklyn Botanic Garden and the Prospect Park Zoo.

Leisurely Walk with The City View
BROOKLYN BRIDGE PARK

This waterfront park stretches for 1.3 miles along the East River and offers stunning views of the Manhattan skyline. It features several piers with recreational activities, including a beach, volleyball courts, and a roller skating rink.

Iconic Sunset Walk
SHORE ROAD PARK

Shore Road Park is a waterfront park located in the Bay Ridge neighborhood of Brooklyn. It offers stunning views of the New York Harbor and the Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge. The park features a playground, bike path, fitness area and more.
